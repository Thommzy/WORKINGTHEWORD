FREE INDEED!

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on May 30, 2018
I took ill in February and I got treated for ulcer, infection and I was able to resume back to the office. In March, I resumed at my new branch in Oregun on the 5th, but inside of me the fatigue, tiredness, headache, and pain is what I kept feeling. I wake up every day tired. I went on leave and came back in April and the symptoms persisted. I began to treat malaria but the headache continued and my BP started rising. I was treated for infection again at this point. On 27th of April, I was very feverish and the doctor had to admit me. I spent 7 days in the hospital and I was treated for typhoid and malaria again. I was placed on anti-hypertensive drug, my BP dropped and I went into crisis, the drug was stopped. I was discharged and referred to another hospital to see three different consultants because of the diagnosis of thyroid disorder, hepatitis and pernicious anaemia I was given. I was consumed by fear, sleep became a problem and I was placed on sleeping tablets. I started seeing the consultants by the first weekend in May and I was directed to do several tests and scans. At this point, I started praying not to hear any bad news. I was invited by one of my big sister friend to attend a sisters’ prayer meeting at Oregun. I met my aunty that I had lost contact with Aunty Sola- Pastor Mrs Olatunji. We spoke and she requested that I come to the church and see Pastor Olatunji. I visited on a Wednesday and he took me through the Word of God. He showed me my freedom in the scriptures and who I am in Christ. The ministers prayed and I had an encounter I have never had before. The spirit of fear tormenting me left me and I was not the same again. Hallelujah. I received a new spirit of life from my creator. I became whole again. I went to see my doctors on Friday 25th and Saturday 26th and all my results did not show anything wrong with me. All the previous diagnosis gone. I have peace in my soul now. I sleep without drugs and I’m not afraid of anything again. No more heart palpitations and BP is back to normal. Praise God with me because this is my testimony and I want to encourage anyone in fear or despair that the name of JESUS is a strong tower, the righteous runs into it and He is saved and THE WORD WORKS! I thank God for Seed of End Time Bible Church for the WORD. The word is a health to my flesh. Praise the LORD!!
