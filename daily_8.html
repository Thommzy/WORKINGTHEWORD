LOOKING TO JESUS

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on June 19, 2018
“Looking unto Jesus, the author and the finisher of our faith who for the joy that was set before Him endured the cross, despising the shame, and is set down at the right hand of the throne of God”- Hebrews 12:2

God never promised us in His Word that we will be free of challenges and tribulations in this world. In fact, He was loving enough to provide for His own children a way out of tribulations and challenges. That solution is Jesus and if you are reading this devotional today, that is the reason why you are still living. Jesus Christ came and overcame all challenges for you and I that there is none that should swallow us up anymore. “These things I have spoken unto you, that in me ye might have peace. In the world ye shall have tribulation: but be of good cheer, I (Jesus) have overcome the world”- John16:33




Faith in Jesus Christ is God’s requirement for receiving His free gift of salvation. Faith in Jesus Christ is faith in what He has done for you. In other words, faith in Jesus Christ is God’s requirement for receiving His free gift of health, peace, prosperity, protection and all that is in Salvation. Faith in Jesus Christ is your requirement to be exempted from the threats of life.



So look away from your fears, look away from the challenges, pain, struggles and disappointments and look to Jesus the author and the finisher of your faith. Looking unto Him is constantly fixing your gaze on Him and all that He has done for you and not on the problems. When the storms are raging, look unto Jesus. When it seems as though nothing is working, look unto Jesus. When what you desire is not what you see, look unto Jesus. He endured the cross so that you will not endure challenges. Be of good cheer, He has overcome for you. Rest in this truth today.


Confession

I rest in the finished works of Christ for my life. I rest in God’s unfailing love that created an escape route for me from all the challenges of life. As I look unto Jesus every day, I decree a change in every area I am struggling with and I receive my victory in Jesus name. Amen!
