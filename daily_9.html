THE CREATIVE POWER OF WORDS

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on May 24, 2018
“And God saw everything that He had made, and behold, it was very good”- Genesis 1:31

In the beginning, the Bible says that the earth was without form and void and darkness covered the face of the earth. God looked at the situation and said to Himself, “this can change, this can be better” and He said...” There are lot of “And God said... Let there be... And it was so” recorded in chapter 1. God created the world with His words. He spoke light to be by His words. He spoke waters to appear by His words, he spoke firmaments, grasses, fruits, living creatures by His words. God spoke the world to exist by His words.




The state of the earth at the beginning could be the state in which believers find themselves in today. You might be saying to yourself and even out loud, nothing is working; I need more finances to meet my needs, I need children to nurture, I need peace in the stormy situation...But I want to ask you today, what are you saying? God did not create the world by the works of His hands but by the words of His mouth. He spoke everything he wanted to see; the exact way He wanted it to be. He did not complain about the situation, He changed it by His words. What are you saying to your situation? You can also rise up and say, “Light be! Money, be! Peace, be! You can say what you want and have it exactly the way you want it.



Why? Because you are created in the exact image and likeness of God. Genesis 1:26- “And God said, let us (God the father, son and Holy Spirit) make man (you and I) in our image, after our likeness: and let them have dominion...” You are created exactly like God and can do exactly what He can do. “You shall decree a thing and it shall be established”. Do not talk about your situation, but talk to your situation and you shall have dominion. Let your circumstances hear your words every time. And the Word of God has assured you that whatever you decree shall be established.


Confession:

I am made in the image and likeness of God. My words carry power and the capacity to change any situation. I command a change in every area that I am struggling with in Jesus name. Henceforth, I speak only to situations and not about them. I walk in victory every day. Amen!
