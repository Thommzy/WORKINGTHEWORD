LIVE IN THE TRUTH OF YOUR RIGHTEOUSNESS

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on May 31, 2018

“For He made Him who knew no sin to be sin for us, that we might become the righteousness of God in Him”-2 Corinthians 5:21 (NKJV)




If you are reading this and you still think or believe that you can be righteous by your works- attitudes and holy conducts, then you have not fully accepted the gospel of Grace. Righteousness is a gift not perfection in your works. The qualification for your righteousness is the Blood of Jesus shed for all your sins before you even thought of committing them. We can boast that we are the righteousness of God in Christ because the Word says so. The more we believe and accept this truth, the stronger the reality is in our lives and the more your earthly life adjusts to it.



The Bible says in Romans 14:17- “The kingdom of God is righteousness, peace and joy in the Holy Ghost”. This is a progression. If we truly desire to live in joy, we must have peace and to have peace, we must have an ‘everyday functioning reality’ of righteousness. It is not in what we do but who we are. This truth must be established in your soul before you can have real rest and peace. Tell the devil enough of tormenting you constantly with guilt and condemnation.


We must be so established in our righteousness that we do not feel defeated even in our weaknesses. We don’t have to live in self rejection and self- hatred when we do wrong any longer. Joyce Meyer said in her book “Conflict- free living” that we are righteous, not because we never make a mistake but because Jesus never made one. Live today and everyday in the truth of your righteousness for that is who you truly are.




Confession

I count myself as righteous because God says so. Because of this, I experience peace and joy every day of my life. I am free of guilt and condemnation. This is the reality I choose to live in today and forever. Amen.
