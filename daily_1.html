LIVE IN POWER

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on June 1, 2018
Therefore we are buried with him by baptism into death: that like as Christ was raised up from the dead by the glory of the Father, even so we also should walk in newness of life.-Romans 6:4

As a believer, you actually have residing in you permanently the same new life God gave Jesus when He raised Him from the dead. The old sinner you once were has died. You’ve become a new creation on the inside that never existed before. The resurrection life of God is at work in you quickening your mortal body.




But sin, disobedience and living a selfish, carnal life will keep that resurrection life from flowing out. Sin will separate you from the power of God, even though you’re born again. Resurrection life will lay dormant in you if you walk in sin. The Bible says in Ephesians 6:23-24 “And be renewed in the spirit of your mind; And that ye put on the new man which after God is created in righteousness and true holiness”.



You can’t overcome sin by trying to stop sinning. You overcome it by walking after the new life God has put within you, by spending time in the Word and in prayer. As you do that, the Spirit of God will strengthen you and enable you to have victory over that sin. The Holy Spirit will not automatically help you to subdue those old fleshly habits of yours on His own. He’ll wait for you to take the initiative and step right in to help you just when you need Him. Then He will give you the strength that you need to follow through on your decision to the end. He will teach you how to walk in the new life that is on the inside of you. Take the first step today by asking for His help.


confession

Lord, I desire to live by this new life every day. This is a decision of my heart; I put down the dictates of sin. I declare myself dead to sin. In Jesus’ Name, I will spend time in prayer and in the Word today. As I do, I believe that the Holy Spirit will help me and I receive it in Jesus name. Amen
