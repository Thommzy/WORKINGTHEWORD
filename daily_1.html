DRINK DEEPLY WITH JOY

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on May 15, 2018
“With Joy you will drink deeply from the fountain of salvation”- Isaiah 12:3 (NLT)

Every single day, we have something that we are looking up unto God for; an area of need that we want to be met or a problem that we desperately need to be solved.




Everything that we need has been made available in the deep fountain of Salvation. Romans 1:16 says “For I am unashamed of the gospel of Christ for it is the power of God unto salvation, to everyone that believeth...”



The Greek word for salvation is ‘Soteria’ meaning nothing missing or broken in your life. It includes health, healing, peace, wealth, prosperity, deliverance, safety etc.


However, it is with joy that you draw out your healing, prosperity, peace, riches and every other thing you desire. It means that you joy is the tool you use to access salvation and without that tool, the benefits of salvation cannot be yours. So, receiving the Word of God for a situation or challenge and standing on it is not enough but remaining in joy.




Joy connotes cheerfulness, gladness, rejoicing, calmness, fullness and happiness. You need to be intentional about being all of these which might require you to get rid of toxic and unhappy people around you, toxic thoughts, emotions and decisions. Rejoice in the Lord ALWAYS and again I say unto you, REJOICE!


Let your heart be glad and you are sure to get the devil off you because he cannot stand the atmosphere of joy. Your victory is sure!
