Pre- marital counselling questions for intending couples

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on June 13, 2018

1.In your opinion, what is God’s will concerning marriage? (with scriptures}
2.What are the reasons for marriage in your own opinion? Cite scriptures
3.Is there any link between societal ills and marriage? Explain with bible references
4.Do a SWOT Analysis: Strengths and weaknesses of one another
5.What weaknesses would you want your fiancée /fiancé to work on and change before marriage?
6.What weaknesses would you want to work on and change before marriage?
7.What strength would you want your fiancé/fiancée to acquire before marriage?
8.What strength would you want to acquire before marriage?
9.What are the qualities of an ideal woman- Man
10.What are the qualities of an ideal man- Woman
11.What will put you off from a man?- Woman
12.What will put you off from a woman? Man
13.What are your expectations for this marriage?
14.What is your perspective of marriage/ what do you think marriage is for?
16.Why do you want to get married?
17.What do you think marriage should do for you?
18.What are you missing now as a single?
19.What gap will your getting married bridge?
20.What stories did you grow up with concerning marriage?
21.Have you discussed your partner with your nuclear family?
22.What are their comments?
23.Why do you think divorce is increasing?
24.What are your dreams- have you discussed them with one another?
25.Are there any misgivings/ comments about each other yet?
26.What is your vision of this union?
27.Are there any secret fears about the future of this union?
28.Are you ready to endure when expectations are not met? Explain
29.Are there secrets of the past that must be revealed? (Past relationships, health/ parental challenges)
30.Have you met one another’s family/ close friends?
31.Do you like everyone in the family of your fiancé? Do they like you?
32.Are your parents living together?
33.How would you describe the marriage of your parents?
34.Are there any areas of their marriage you want to experience? Explain
35.Are their areas of their marital life you do not want to re live? Explain
36.In your opinion, what are the secrets of a successful marriage?
37.How many books have you read on marriage?
38.Have you had a serious relationship before? Why did it break up?
39.Have you told one another about past relationships?
40.Have you proposed to someone before, woman? If yes, why didn’t it work out?
41.Have you been proposed to before or had proposed to someone before? If yes, explain
42.Is there any marital experience you witnessed before that gave you pain? Explain
43.While growing up, what are your secret fears about marriage?
44.What in your own opinion should be the foundation of an enduring/successful union?
45.When did you give your life to Jesus as Lord and Saviour? State the circumstances surrounding it
46.Have you been baptized by water immersion? If no why?
47.Have you been baptized by Holy Spirit with the evidence of speaking other tongues? If No why?
48.How will you describe your spiritual life, that is, your daily relationship with the LORD?
49.Can you cite any role model for your marriage? Why did you pick your choice?
50.How many children would you want to have?
51.Where would you want to settle after marriage/ Nigeria or outside? Why?
52.What is your impression about in laws? Pests or helps?
53.What is your comment on marriages where there are no children?
54.How will you feel when there is a delay in conception after marriage?
55.Were you married before? If yes, give details of it.
56.Would you say that your previous marriage(s) were successful? If yes how? And if no, why?
57.What mistakes from the past would you avoid/correct in this new marriage?
58.What nice lessons would you bring from your previous marriage into the present?
59.Will you allow him to be the head?
60.Would you rather stay unmarried?
