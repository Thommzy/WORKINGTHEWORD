LOVE OF GOD CASTS FEAR OUT

Posted by Pastor O.O OLATUNJI on May 14, 2018

Good evening saints of God. I am a saint, and so are you if you are also washed by the Blood of the Lamb slain from the foundation of the world (Rev13:8). It's worth repeating again and again, keep saying what God says about you; don't stop saying it. For instance, there is only one way to living above fear which is the number one enemy of man: acknowledge, believe, meditate, and say how much God loves you.



1John4:18NKJV "There is no fear in love; but perfect love casts out fear, because fear involves torment. But he who fears has not been made perfect in love. We love Him because He first loved us"


It is the love of God accepted and worked out in our lives that dispels fear and anxiety. I have also found out that it is through the love of God that we become more than conquerors.


Rom 8:37" Yet in all these things we are more than conquerors through Him who loved us."

Please pay attention to this scripture that says I am more than a conqueror not because I am a good fighter or a church goer or a prayer warrior or astute at fasting and giving, but because God loves me.


Eph3:17-19 NKJV "that Christ may dwell in your hearts through faith; that you, being rooted and grounded in love, may be able to comprehend with all the saints what is the width and length and depth and height— to know the love of Christ which passes knowledge; that you may be filled with all the fullness of God.

Another mind boggling revelation about the love of God is that it passes knowledge. That God in the flesh (1Tim3:16) would be responsible for all my sins and curses, passes knowledge and understanding. Again we see in earlier post that we are filled with the fullness of God, to the extent that we know that God loves us. Take time to ponder/muse upon the truth of God's love and your life will never remain the same again


1Co4:19-20TLB "But I will come, and soon, if the Lord will let me, and then I’ll find out whether these proud men are just big talkers or whether they really have God’s power. The Kingdom of God is not just talking; it is living by God’s power.


1Co2:4-5NKJV "And my speech and my preaching were not with persuasive words of human wisdom, but in demonstration of the Spirit and of power, that your faith should not be in the wisdom of men but in the power of God."

If a believer has the WORD and don't know how to walk and function in GOD'S power, frustration may be the end result. Why are things not happening as promised me? He will continually lament! No wonder, Paul by the HolySpirit, asked Timothy to stir up the gift of God in him (2Tim 1:6-7: that is, fan it into flame/ember). And if a believer has, and knows how to live in God's power but has no word, He may end up proud and dangerous. There may not be character and temperance to balance things out. The wisdom of God therefore is for the New Testament believer to balance both The Word and Kingdom power


1John3:1-2 TLB "See how very much our heavenly Father loves us, for he allows us to be called his children—think of it—and we really are! But since most people don’t know God, naturally they don’t understand that we are his children. Yes, dear friends, we are already God’s children, right now, and we can’t even imagine what it is going to be like later on. But we do know this, that when he comes we will be like him, as a result of seeing him as he really is."


1John3:1-2AMP "SEE WHAT [an incredible] quality of love the Father has given (shown, bestowed on) us, that we should [be permitted to] be named and called and counted the children of God! And so we are! The reason that the world does not know (recognize, acknowledge) us is that it does not know (recognize, acknowledge) Him.


Beloved, we are [even here and] now God's children; it is not yet disclosed (made clear) what we shall be [hereafter], but we know that when He comes and is manifested, we shall [as God's children] resemble and be like Him, for we shall see Him just as He [really] is.
