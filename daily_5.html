FEED YOUR HEART WITH THE WORD

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on May 18, 2018
“The upright (honourable, intrinsically good) man out of the good treasure (stored) in his heart produces what is upright (honourable and intrinsically good), and the evil man out of the evil storehouse bring forth that which is depraved (wicked and intrinsically evil); for out of the abundance (overflow) of the heart a man speaks.- - Luke 6:45 (AMP)

Is it not interesting to know that your heart is a store house? You might need to take some time to ponder on that statement. The fact that your heart is a store house signifies that it can either be full or empty. Also, what comes out of your store house is what was put there originally. One cannot walk into a store house of fishes and expect to get bread loaves. The same way your heart cannot produce all the things you desire if they are not stored up there. You cannot expect your heart to bring out health when you have only fed it with news of sickness and diseases.




Note the latter part of the scripture above. It says out of the abundance of the heart, the mouth speaks. It used the word ‘abundance’. It could have been out of the contents of the heart, the mouth speaks; but it is out of the abundance of the contents of the heart, the mouth speaks. This means that at every point in time, there is an abundance of either good or bad in your heart. This brings us to the question, ‘What are you feeding your heart with?”



Philippians 4:8 tells us exactly what to feed our hearts on. Whatever is true, honest, just, pure, lovely, of good report and if there be any virtue in it, “Think on these things”. And I make bold to say that The Word of God is true, honest, just, pure, lovely, of good report and has a lot of virtues in it that cannot be explored in a lifetime. Constantly and consistently feed your mind on the Word of God for everything you need flows from how much of it you have inside you. Keep your store house full of the Word to an overflow.


Confession

My heart is my store house and is a store house of only good things. I permit only the Word of God to be retained in my life. I refuse the news, experiences and testimonies of the world to take root in my mind.
