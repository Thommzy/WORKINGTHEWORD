CONFIDENCE IN GOD

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on June 16, 2018
“And this is the confidence (the assurance, the privilege of boldness) which we have in Him: (we are sure) that if we ask anything (make any request) according to His will (in agreement with His own plan), He listens to and hears us. And if (since) we (positively) know that He listens to us in whatever we ask, we also know (with settled and absolute knowledge) that we have (granted us as our present possessions) the requests made of Him.-1 John 5:14-15 (AMP)

The key to having your prayers answered is having absolute confidence in God, in His Word, and in His unfailing ability to bring His word concerning you and everything that is yours to pass. Anytime you pray to God, you should be fully convinced that He hears you every time and He hears your requests. And if He hears you, whatever you request is already your possession; whether you see it physically or not.




This is what will cause you not to waver, even when you do not see the manifestation of what you have asked as quickly as you want it to appear. You must believe that you have received the answers to your requests the moment you pray. “Therefore I say unto you, whatsoever things ye desire, when ye pray, believe that you receive them and ye shall have them”- Mark 11:24.



The key to your confidence is your understanding that God’s Word never fails and cannot return to Him void until it has accomplished that which it was sent to. This means that you have to do whatever it takes to build your confidence in the Word of God concerning your health, finances, peace and so on. The next time you pray, believe that you have already received it, thank God for it and stay in confidence.


Confession

I have confidence in what the Word of God says concerning my life and my confidence does not waver. I believe that I receive answers each time I pray and I thank God for it. Amen!
