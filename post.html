Living in The Word

Posted by Pastor O.O OLATUNJI on May 14, 2018

Welcome to this article, favoured ones (Ps5:12; Job10:12). Trust your life is running according to God’s agenda, If it is not so, then we have a right to query/question/challenge it. My counsel to you this week is to make it a fear-free one. This week, with the weapons of our warfare (2Co10:3-4): The Word of God (Jer23:29; Heb4:12);


The Name of Jesus (Lule10:17; Phil2:9-11); the blood of Jesus (Rev12:11; Ex12:12-13; Zech 9:11-12), make sure you eliminate doubt (Matt21:21), unbelief (Mark9:24; Ro4:20; Heb3:19) & fear (Luke12:32; 2Tim1:7; Mark4:40) from your heart/mind/soul. How can this be done with all the flurry of fearful news, founded and unfounded flowing from the world these days?




Let's us see how Jesus helped Jairus managed his fear when it came from his own house
Luke8:50 nkjv "But when Jesus heard it, He answered him, saying, “Do not be afraid; only believe, and she will be made well.”


What about Elisha’s servant?

2Kings6:15-17 nkjv "And when the servant of the man of God arose early and went out, there was an army, surrounding the city with horses and chariots. And his servant said to him, “Alas, my master! What shall we do?” So he answered, “Do not fear, for those who are with us are more than those who are with them.” And Elisha prayed, and said, “Lord, I pray, open his eyes that he may see.” Then the Lord opened the eyes of the young man, and he saw. And behold, the mountain was full of horses and chariots of fire all around Elisha."


Ps112:7-8 nkjv "He will not be afraid of evil tidings; his heart is steadfast, trusting in the Lord. his heart is established; he will not be afraid, until he sees his desire upon his enemies.

One way of keeping fear out of your path, is to mind what you pay attention to every day. Stop paying attention to your body symptoms (Jonah2:9KJV) or doctor's report; stop paying attention to unpaid bills and the mounting needs; stop paying attention to the troubled/distressed marriage; stop paying attention to the dreadful traffic/work/boss/colleagues. Start paying attention to what God says in His Word. Get your concordance out and start pulling out seeds (the Word of God Like 8:11) from the 66 bags of indestructible and incorruptible seeds 1Pe1:23-24 (Genesis to Revelation: word of God) and start sowing with your thoughts and mouth into the soil of your heart (Mark4:3, 26-29), and change the situations.



Pro4:20-22 nkjv "My son, give attention to my words; Incline your ear to my sayings. Do not let them depart from your eyes; Keep them in the midst of your heart; for they are life to those who find them, and health to all their flesh.

God our Father has told us what to give attention to: HIS WORD. Period! Just obey this so simple commandment and you will be safe, always. He did not recommend any other thing. If you look at any other thing or person instead of the Word, you are strictly on your own.





2Co3:18 (Darby) But we all, looking on the glory of the LORD, with unveiled face, are transformed according to the same image from glory to glory, even as by the LORD the Spirit"


This is one of the most important scriptures. Simply, the HolySpirit says you sure will metamorphosis into the exact Word of God (glory of God) you keep looking at as Jacob's animals kept looking at the green poplar tree and produce after it (law of reproduction: Gen30:37-43. Everything/one/produces after itself).


If you keep looking unto healing scriptures (Ex15:26; 23:25-26; Deu7:15, Psalm103:3,107:20; Isaiah53:4-5; Matt8:16-17; 1Pe2:24) you will produce and enjoy healing/ health. If you keep looking at peace scriptures, (1saiah9:6, Eph2:14, John14:27) you will produce calmness serenity in your soul. (Isaiah30:15, 32:17) If you keep looking at prosperity scriptures (2Co8:9; Deu8:18; Ps66:12; Job22:24-25; Ro8:16-17; Luke15:31), you will produce and enjoy financial/economic freedom/increase. If you keep seeing scriptures on safe sleep you will have restful and peaceful nights, free of anxiety, panic, fear and sleep paralysis. (Pro1;33; 3:24-25; Ps3:5;4:8; Ps127:2; Jer31:26) . There are some conditions though: one, remove the veil of religion, law, doubt, fear, unbelief, tradition, culture, superstition, and put on GRACE. Two, even when it appears things are not changing, just keep looking; don't stop looking.



LET US PRAY


I pray over someone today as one under heaven's command for such a time like this, that all the intimidating tactics/systems of your enemies be shattered in Jesus name. I pray that all situations bringing dread, fear and panic around you dried up to their roots in Jesus name. I decree that all triggers/influences of fear around you be consumed by fire. I speak to the source where your enemies draw occult/demonic power, wisdom, and strength against your wellbeing /welfare, I command it to dry up now in Jesus name. You are blessed and that is irreversible by any demonic force in Jesus name
