CONFESSION OF FREEDOM FROM ALL EVILS

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on May 30, 2018

I confess to my deliverance & redemption from all evils and works of darkness. Jdg 6:23 And the LORD said unto him, Peace be unto thee; fear not: thou shalt not die. I refuse to fear, because I shall not die but live to declare the works of the lord in the land of the living. Joh 17:15 I pray not that thou shouldest take them out of the world, but that thou shouldest keep them from the evil. I declare that I am kept away from all evils by my heavenly Father. Gen 48:16 The Angel which redeemed me from all evil, bless the lads; and let my name be named on them, and the name of my fathers Abraham and Isaac; and let them grow into a multitude in the midst of the earth. All the days of my life I am redeemed from all evils in Jesus name




Gen 28:15 And, behold, I am with thee, and will keep thee in all places whither thou goest, and will bring thee again into this land; for I will not leave thee, until I have done that which I have spoken to thee of. God has promised to keep me from all evils in every place I step upon, In any country where I stay Psa 121:5-8 The LORD is thy keeper: the LORD is thy shade upon thy right hand. The sun shall not smite thee by day, nor the moon by night. The LORD shall preserve thee from all evil: he shall preserve thy soul. The LORD shall preserve thy going out and thy coming in from this time forth, and even for evermore. Every day of my earthly life, I am preserved from all evils. The Lord preserves my soul; he preserves my going out and my coming in. His eyes are all over me. The name of the Lord is my strong tower to keep me safe every day and time of my life



Psa 91:9-10 Because thou hast made the LORD, which is my refuge, even the most High, thy habitation; There shall no evil befall thee, neither shall any plague come nigh thy dwelling I dwell in the secret place of the Almighty and I abide in His shadow. God Himself is my habitation so I can boldly say, that no evil shall come near me neither shall any plague come near me in Jesus name His angels are given charge over me to keep me in all my ways lest I dash my foot against the stone. They encamp round about me to deliver me, in all my ways. Pro 1:33 But whoso hearkeneth unto me shall dwell safely, and shall be quiet from fear of evil.

Praise the Lord, I am quiet from fear of evil; I dwell safely

Luk 21:18 and Luk 12:7 But there shall not an hair of your head perish. But even the very hairs of your head are all numbered. Fear not therefore: ye are of more value than many sparrows I agree with the word of God that not a strand of all my hair that my heavenly father numbered shall perish


Joh 10:28-29 And I give unto them eternal life; and they shall never perish, neither shall any man pluck them out of my hand. My Father, which gave them me, is greater than all; and no man is able to pluck them out of my Father's hand. My father is greater than all the men and women of the underworld. I have received eternal life so I shall never perish. My name is off the register of those earmarked for perishing.

Psa 125:1-3 They that trust in the LORD shall be as mount Zion, whichcannot be removed, but abideth for ever. As the mountains are round about Jerusalem, so the LORD is round about his people from henceforth even for ever. For the rod of the wicked shall not rest upon the lot of the righteous; lest the righteous put forth their hands unto iniquity

Glory be to God, I trust in the Lord and I am as mount Zion which can never be removed; I abide in God for ever with no fear of evil. God is round about me and all my children in Nigeria and abroad. I declare that never will the rod of the wicked rest upon my lot/land/marriage/health/finances.

Pro 12:21& 28 There shall no evil happen to the just: but the wicked shall be filled with mischief. In the way of righteousness is life; and in the pathway thereof there is no death.





Because I am the righteousness of God in Christ Jesus, no evil shall befall me and no death can be found in my path except life. Gal 1:4 Who gave himself for our sins, that he might deliver us from this present evil world, according to the will of God and our Father: I declare that Jesus came to die for me and by so doing has redeemed me from all evil. He rescued me from this evil & wicked world 1Jn 5:18 We know that whosoever is born of God sinneth not; but he that is begotten of God keepeth himself, and that wicked one toucheth him not. I am begotten of God, I declare that the wicked one touches me not in Jesus name. Pro 19:23 The fear of the LORD tendeth to life: and he that hath it shall abide satisfied; he shall not be visited with evil. I declare that I fear the Lord therefore I shall never be visited with evil all the days of my life. 2Ti 4:18 And the Lord shall deliver me from every evil work, and will preserve me unto his heavenly kingdom: to whom be glory for ever and ever. Amen. I declare that till Gods kingdom, the Lord shall deliver me from every evil work


Isa 54:14-15& 17 In righteousness shalt thou be established: thou shalt be far from oppression; for thou shalt not fear: and from terror; for it shall not come near thee. Behold, they shall surely gather together, but not by me: whosoever shall gather together against thee shall fall for thy sake.No weapon that is formed against thee shall prosper; and every tongue that shall rise against thee in judgment thou shalt condemn. This is the heritage of the servants of the LORD, and their righteousness is of me, saith the LORD.


I confess with my mouth and believe in my heart that I am the righteousness of God, therefor I am established on this truth and now I am far from oppression and terror for I shall not fear, When wicked people gather against me they shall fall for my sake. My heritage as a son/daughter of God is that no weapon formed against me shall prosper and every tongue that rises against me in judgement I shall condemn. I declare I am blessed, praise the lord Halleluyah!
