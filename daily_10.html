THE SPIRITUAL MAN

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on May 29, 2018
“But the natural man does not receive the things of the Spirit of God for they are foolishness to him; nor can he know them, because they are spiritually discerned. But he who is spiritual judges all things, yet he himself is rightly judged by no one”- 1 Corinthians 2:14-15 (NKJV)

Believers are partakers of the divine nature that is in Christ. They are people that believe and convinced daily that Jesus Christ is Lord over all. It is easy to identify someone as a believer simply because he or she goes to church or is kind to the poor. But being a believer is not just being a Christian but being spiritual (led by the Spirit) in your thoughts, words and actions.




The spiritual man or woman is a regenerated person in Christ. One who constantly submits to the Holy Spirit to be rebuilded, remoulded and renewed. One that is willing to let go of old habits, thinking patterns, attitudes, beliefs and so on. A believer is spiritually minded i.e. they think only the thoughts of God; not ruled or swayed easily by what they see but by what God has said. A believer lives by the Spirit of God and strives to follow the leading of the Spirit of God in everything.



One you have identified with Christ and are born again, you are already regenerated because you have been given the Holy Spirit and a new nature. There has to be a daily impact of the Spirit of God in your life; a daily infusion of the Spirit of God. You are spiritual when you are renewed daily by transforming your mind with the Word of God.


Go into the Word of God daily. Get to know who you are, what you have and how your life should be moulded. “...be ye transformed by the renewing of your mind, that ye may prove what is that good, and acceptable, and perfect, will of God”. Renewing is continuous restoration to freshness; to make new spiritually. As you do this, you begin to notice changes in you; your heart, thoughts, words, actions, habits and attitudes. The old way of life will gradually fall of and you look more and more like Christ. Decide to live by the Spirit of God today.


Confession:

I decide to live my life by the leading of the Spirit of God. I align my spirit to the Spirit of God to receive instructions and directions for my life daily. Amen!
