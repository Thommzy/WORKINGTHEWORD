FRUITFULNESS BY GOD’S WORD

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on June 11, 2018
I had been married for 8 years with no fruit of the womb. We had done all that we can and my husband had earlier confessed to be sexually inactive. I was introduced to the pastor of Seed of End time Bible Church through a woman that raised me who happens to be his mother-in-law. I reside in Ekiti State and each time the pastor came visiting he would share the Word of God with me, showing me who I am and what I have in Christ.

I was invited to a quarterly program (Word Feast) in August 2017. It was a 5- day program and on the last day, the pastor prayed and anointed everyone looking for the fruit of the womb. He prayed for me and anointed me and I went back to Ekiti State. 3 months later, I became pregnant and as I’m sharing this testimony, I am 7 months pregnant. To God be all the glory. The Word works.

-B.A
