CONFESSION TO ATTRACT THE RIGHT MARRIAGE PATRNER

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on May 30, 2018

With the word of God and the power of the Spirit, I condition my spirit to attract and lead me to the right spouse in Jesus name. My heavenly Father, Your Word says that it is not good for a man to be alone and so you make a help mate for man, your creature. The word also says that all good and perfect gifts come from above; therefore, I confess that I am not going to remain single. Because marriage is your idea, I receive my portion in marriage.




I disallow this condition of living alone to continue, for you say that two are better than one, for they have results for their labour. You also said that whatsoever I disallow in my life is going to be what disallowed n heaven is. Father your word says marriage is honourable, so I choose to get married this year and receive my portion of your favour, glory and honour. I shall not allow satan to keep my marriage partner away from me further than this year in Jesus name. Because it is the will of God concerning me in Christ Jesus, I shall not lose anything that God has intended for me (my partner inclusive) and given to me in Jesus name.



Lord, Your Word tells me that none shall lack her mate and also that no good thing shall He withhold from the upright I place my feet on these verses and, I claim them as mine. Since I am the righteousness of God in Christ, I therefore declare that I shall not lack my mate in Jesus name. Father, I receive a change of status from single to marriedaccording to Your Word which says, “My land shall be called married.” In my choice of partner, I shall not be unequally yoked together with an unbeliever and I also declare that I shall not lack God’s direction in my choice of a future partner for the Word of God says, “My ears shall hear a word behind me telling me this is the way, walk in it” By the help of the Holy Spirit, I will diligently obey God as He speaks to me concerning my marriage in Jesus name..


Lord, I confess that the path of my marriage partner and I are crossing now Father, I know that your thoughts towards me are thoughts of peace not evil and to give me an expected end.Therefore, I declare that I shall end well and be happily married in Jesus name. I confess that I am long suffering, I am kind, I am not envious, I do not parade myself, I am not puffed up, I do not behave rudely, I do not seek my own, I am not easily provoked, I think no evil, I do not rejoice in iniquity but rejoice in the truth, I bear all things, believe all things, hope all things, and I endure all things.I receive the true love of God that never fails in Jesus name. I confess the same over my marriage partner, whoever he/she is and wherever he/she is in Jesus name I confess over myself and my future partner that we possess the fruit of the Spirit by faith which is love, joy, peace, long suffering, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness and self control in Jesus name. I totally consecrate myself to God and his plans and renounce all my own plans and human standards. Not my will but yours be find O Lord regarding my spouse. The peace of God that transcends all understanding shall continue to be my experience in Jesus name.




SCRIPTURE REFERENCES TO MEDITATE UPON
