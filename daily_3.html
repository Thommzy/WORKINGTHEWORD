CONFIDENCE IN GOD’S LOVE

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on June 2, 2018
“And we have known and believed the love that God has to us...”-1 John 4:16a

You may know that God cares for you and loves you, and that He demonstrated His love for you by dying on the cross for you, but do you believe it? It is one thing to know about the love that God has for you, but another thing to actually believe it in your heart. It is not theory, it should be real to you and you should have confidence in this truth.




Sometimes we are slow in believing God’s love for us when we are going through difficult times. We sometimes even ask ourselves why God would allow you go through pain or struggle. But God wants you to know and believe that He loves you in the midst of the sickness in your body, when that job you have been expecting is slow in coming, when the relationship failed or when your child is being stubborn.



In the midst of the pain, He wants you to know and believe that He is still on your side defending and taking care of you. And if God, the most powerful being in the universe, is for you, can anyone or anything come against you successfully?


Perhaps you feel you have wronged God and that is why He is punishing you. But be assured today, He will never be angry with you. Neither will He condemn you. All of His anger against your sins was laid upon Jesus at the cross of Calvary. So He wants you to know and believe that He will never punish you because Jesus was punished for you. Don’t feel unworthy of His love, come boldly to His throne of grace to obtain mercy and find grace. (Hebrews 4:16)


Confession

I rest today in the unfailing and unchanging love that God has toward me. I lay every guilt and condemnation at His feet and receive His peace and love into the very core of my being in Jesus name. Amen.
