RENEW YOUR MIND, TRANSFORM YOUR LIFE

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on May 15, 2018

“I beseech you therefore, brethren, by the mercies of God, that ye present your bodies a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable unto God, which is your reasonable service. And be not conformed to this world: but be ye transformed by the renewing of your mind, that ye may prove what is that good, and acceptable, and perfect will of God” - Romans 12:1-2




The question you need to ask yourself is do I want to transform my life? Do I want to live better? Do I desire to have a better quality of life? You already have an answer. The way provided by Paul, by the Spirit of God to improve or upgrade the quality of your life is by the renewing of your mind. God has given you your mind for your transformation. If you do not want to conformed to this world, use their strategies or have their results, you have to renew your mind.



What does it mean to renew your mind? It means to re-organize, refresh or re-program your mind which is the seat of your thoughts, emotions, feelings resulting in newer actions and attitudes.


God’s greatest desire for us as his children is to live victorious and prosperous lives. “Beloved I wish above all things that you prosper and be in health even as thy soul prospers” - 3 John 1:2.




One of the reasons why many are still struggling is because they do not know what has been made available unto them and their inability to appropriate to themselves what has been made available to them. You need to accept and process all that has been done for you and begin to walk in the reality of it. “But without thy mind would I do nothing; that thy benefit should not be as it were of necessity, but willingly- Philemon 1:14


How do you renew your mind? Read, study and meditate on the Word of God daily. Spend time discovering who you really are, what you can do, what you have, and where you belong. When you discover all these, there is no limit in your mind to create the life you desire. The life you live is largely determined by the content and quality of your mind. Spend time in the Word of God today!



Confession

My life is transformed daily as I renew my mind in the Word of God. I think only God’s thoughts and I say only what God says. I yield my mind only to the power of the Holy Spirit in Jesus name. Amen.
