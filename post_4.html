WRONG THINKING: THE ENEMY OF HEALING

Posted by Pastor O.O OLATUNJI on May 30, 2018

The enemy called sickness is limited to the quality of our thoughts. Our healing/health is directly connected to our thoughts. The quality of our life is rooted in our thoughts. The direction of our health follows our thought patterns, invariably.

Pro23:7 says "For as he thinks in his heart, so is he.“eat and drink!” he says to you, But his heart is not with you.". In the study of my bible, I have found that God our father places high premium on thoughts; even His own thoughts.

Psa92:5 "O Lord, how great are Your works!Your thoughts are very deep. Here we can see the connextion between great works of: parting the red sea; rod turned to serpents, bringing out water from the rock; raising the dead; healing the sick; etc and deep thoughts Jer29:11 nkjv "For I know the thoughts that I think toward you, says the Lord, thoughts of peace and not of evil, to give you a future and a hope." For God to bless us, it has to begin in His thoughts. Since we are created in His image (we are the exact duplication of Him Gen1:26-28; 2Pe1:4 and we are to imitate Him Eph5:1)

Isaiah 55:7-8 "Let the wicked forsake his way, And the unrighteous man his thoughts; Let him return to the Lord, And He will have mercy on him; And to our God, For He will abundantly pardon. “For My thoughts are not your thoughts, Nor are your ways My ways,” says the Lord. God says the reason why you are not receiving your health/healing, my way (the way i established it in the kingdomof heaven) is because you are not thinking my thoughts after me. And as long as we cannot agree in thoughts then we cannot walk together on healing/health, God's way. Amos 3:3. As far as heaven is from the earth, that is as far as your thoughts are from mine Isaiah 55:9-10.

Divine healing from God and your thoughts are inextricably linked If a man is crippled in his thoughts, sure enough, his health will be crippled. And a quick one is: if the word of God is not your only source of information for your healing/health, your thoughts will be un-wholesome. Many have too many sources of in- form-ation (what you put inside is what will form you). When symptoms show up in your body, earthly doctors should not be the first person to call ; it's your heavenly Physician, Jesus Ex15:26: He is with you through His Word: Ro10:6-10. He is our family physician Psalm23:5. Glory to God Hallelujah.

Pro15:26 "The thoughts of the wicked are an abomination to the Lord, But the words of the pure are pleasant." Psalm10:4 "The wicked in his proud countenance does not seek God;God is in none of his thoughts" if GOD is not in your thought, His way of healing/health certainly is not there. I have found out that many believers are too rich or educated to receive GOD'S help of divine healing when they need it. Their money is their first thought at the beginning of an health issue. They want to spend, and so they bypass GOD and His word. And after spending their money, they will still come back to the God they should have started with; and it is a pity, some don't have opportunity to come back to Him. Let's be wise children of God and stop giving up our right to health easily.




Jer4:14 nkjv "O Jerusalem, wash your heart from wickedness, That you may be saved. How long shall your evil thoughts lodge within you?" This is a good question. Jerusalem here is a type of the church/Zion. Evil thoughts are those thoughts outside God's WORD/thoughts. As long as we have thoughts from other sources aside God , Paul says, they become strongholds & wicked/vain/distorted/corrupted images turning into imaginations, because we think in pictures 2Co10:4-5. And you must now deal with that to receive your healing. Many have too many strongholds to pull down or imaginations to cast down: lies that have been spoken over them. Could it be, that the reason for lingering pains in your body is wrong thinking? If yes, then, get rid of it. Straighten out your thoughts with GOD'S WORD/Grace in Jesus NAME. REMEMBER THAT JESUS IS LORD OVER THE CURSE OF FEAR SICKNESS DISEASES DEATH & HIGHPRIEST OVER THE BLESSING OF HEALTH, PEACE, JOY &LIFE Co2:9-10 & Heb3:1
