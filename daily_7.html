NO MORE CONFUSION

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on June 18, 2018
“Then spoke Jesus unto them again saying, I am the light of the world: he that follows me shall not walk in darkness but shall have the light of life”-John 8:12

Have you ever been in a situation where it seems like nothing makes sense to you, nothing is working or you do not know what to do concerning any situation? The word “Darkness” comes from the Greek word “skotia”. It means “physical darkness, i.e. the absence of light” or “spiritual darkness”; it also means “moral blindness”. The Greek concepts of light and dark are very closely related to knowledge and ignorance. This means the verse above says those that choose to follow Jesus shall not walk in ignorance concerning anything; you know all things.




The word translated as "world" is kosmos, which means specifically, the world order. When He says "I am the light of the world," He is not saying that He is part of the world. Instead, He is setting Himself apart from the world; Jesus is the way out of darkness, that is, ignorance. If there is any situation you find yourself and you do not know what to do, command light to be! What you are doing is speaking the solution- that is Jesus to work at that moment.



You have the secrets of life in your spirit and the keys to unlocking mysteries. Nothing should be unknown to you. The Bible says in 1 Corinthians 2:12 “Now we have received, not the spirit of the world, but the spirit which is of God; that we might know the things that are freely given to us of God”. Light shines in darkness and darkness cannot stop it. When you receive light concerning any situation, darkness knows what to do. All you need to do is turn on the light.


Confession

I speak light to be in every area of my life where there is darkness. I refuse to be confused or stranded in life. I always know what to do, I always know what to say. My spirit is alert to receive instructions and directions from the Spirit of God. Amen!
