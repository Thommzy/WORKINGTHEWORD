CONFESSION OF A LIFE OF GRACE

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on June 5, 2018

I declare and confess that in every territory I enter on this plant earth, I am a financial authority in Jesus name
I declare that I am a walking magnet of financial favour and blessings in Jesus name
I begin to obtain grace and favour in the eyes of those that matter to my destiny in Jesus name.
I declare that I am a receiver of financial authority and anointing. I am a master of the law of harvests and the law of receiving in Jesus name
Father I cease your favour and that of men of the earth that matter with my hands in Jesus name I declare that every good withheld from me is restored now in Jesus name
I declare that I am a walking magnet and carrier of favour around the globe. I speak favour; I attract favour; I walk in favour every day of my life and in every place.




I declare that favour is flowing towards my direction today in Jesus name My words are pictures of blessings and favour
I cannot be mistreated or disadvantaged on earth because I belong to the great family of God Father, thank you because the anointing of the Holy Ghost has removed every burden and yoke that the enemies have placed upon my life, career, income and destiny
The anointing stops the activities, operations of, and handles any present enemies in my life Thank you Jesus because miracles are being scheduled for my life today in Jesus name Father I believe that there is nothing the Holy Spirit cannot birth in my life since he raised the messiah, Jesus from the dead and he is in me, with me, for me and with me Jesus was begotten from the dead, so was I with Him.
