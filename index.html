FROM THE CEO’s DESK

Welcome to our website

Every manufacturer of a product has a manual to operate, guide, fix, repair, correct, adjust and possibly replace a missing or malfunctioning part in its product. Every manual starts with strict instructions on how to switch on and use the product. Should the product become malfunctioning or faulty, the manufacturer directs to an authorised dealer only.
Our Father is the manufacturer Ephesian2:10, and Jesus and the church, His body, is God’s authorised dealer on earth. The word of God is the manufacturers manual

No child of God; indeed, no one on earth can enjoy himself without knowing how the manufacturer put him together for an effective functioning.
Salvation came with all the good things that pertain to life and godliness. Salvation repositioned us from the dominated to the one with dominion and authority on earth over the unseen demonic spirits and all the beggarly elements. We are new creatures who never existed before superior to satan and all the dark forces in the universe, including their operations. We have received abundance of grace and the gift of righteousness to reign and rule in this life. We are destined for greatness, to be in charge of our destiny and to have things run in our life just as we want it, laid down specifically by our manufacturer
Our salvation is sure and true. However it has to be real and true to our soul. We have to work it out. In fact the instruction from the father is that His individual sons and daughters should personally work out their own salvation with fear and trembling.

Php 2:12 Wherefore, my beloved, as ye have always obeyed, not as in my presence only, but now much more in my absence, work out your own salvation with fear and trembling.
Only the word worked out can build, grow and mature us; and only by our growth and maturity can we access and make our inheritance real in our earthly life.

Act 20:32 And now, brethren, I commend you to God, and to the word of his grace, which is able to build you up, and to give you an inheritance among all them which are sanctified.

This is the purpose of this ministry: workingtheword. It started about four years ago when we received the vision to go and teach the word God to appropriate the benefits salvation. We thank God for all the testimonies we have recorded. Terminal illnesses have been terminated; barrenness have been overturned. Marriages have been restored. In short, lives have been touched globally.
Our purpose is to increase our circle of influence globally, hence this website We pray men and women; boys and girls will find this ministry relevant to their earthly lining in Jesus name.

Meanwhile, keep living in the blessings of your salvation
