What makes us different

ABOUT WORKING THE WORD

Working the word is an interdenominational in- depth Bible study set up by the Seed of End Time Bible Church to show believers how to work out their salvation. Birthed by the testimony of a woman who had cancer relapse and spread over her body, she got to Seed of End Time Bible Church and consistently worked the word concerning her health for 8 months. She went back to the same doctors and was given a cancer-free result. This programme therefore is anchored on the scripture, Philippians 2:12 (KJV) “Wherefore, my beloved, as ye have always obeyed, not as in my presence only, but now much more in my absence, work out your salvation with fear and trembling”.


Everything a child of God needs to live a successful life and have dominion on earth has been given and made available in Christ. That however, does not automatically guarantee a successful life. A believer needs to know what has been done and appropriate; hold on to the finished works of Christ to continually walk and live in victory.


This is the major reason why Working the Word exists; to show believers who they are in Christ, what they have and the instruments/ weapons they have to walk in victory over the works of the devil. The Word of God is to a believer what a cutlass or a hoe is to a farmer. A believer without the Word of God lacks the instruments to harvest the blessings of God made available in Christ. Working the Word will show you how to take advantage of all the benefits of salvation.


Salvation in Greek is ‘Soteria' which means nothing missing or lacking in the life of a believer. It includes peace, health, healing, prosperity, faith, riches, strength, wholeness, victory and other benefits. Everything you need or desire is in salvation. But Philippians 2:12 tells us to work out your peace, health, prosperity with all seriousness and diligence. If you are in a situation or challenge that you do not like and want to change, all you need is to know what God says about it in His word and you take your freedom. “And ye shall know the truth (the Word of God) and the truth shall make (continuously) you free”. Working the Word will help you and bring you up to the level where all you think are God’s thoughts and all you say are God’s words.


A believer needs to be filled with the Word of God. Your Spirit needs to soak in the Word of God all the time for it is the Word of God in your Spirit that delivers unto you what you desire. Working the Word exists to teach believers how to store up the Word in their hearts and keep them always in their mouths. “This book of the law shall not depart out of thy mouth; but thou shall meditate therein day and night, that thou may observe to do according to all that is written therein: for then thou shall make thy way prosperous and then, thou shall have good success- Joshua 1:8”. This is what ensures our victory.
