RECEIVE YOUR ANSWERS

Posted by DAVID O OLATUNJI on May 19, 2018
“Therefore I say unto you, whatever things you ask when you pray, believe that you receive them and you shall have them”-Mark 11:24

The disciples of Jesus Christ walked with Him and saw Him do great and mighty things. And so many times they were amazed and marvelled at the works of God and they couldn’t hide it. One of the instances was the lesson of the fig tree. A day before, His disciples had seen Him curse a fig tree because it had no fruit on it. And the next day, passing by that same fig tree, they realised that it had dried up from the roots. And they said to Him surprisingly and immediately, he sensed their lack of faith. Jesus dropped a lesson here which is: “When you pray, believe that you receive in that moment; do not doubt and you shall have them”




Prayer is beyond communication to God; it is our weapon of change on the face of the earth. However, the change that you seek is tied to your belief that you receive it when you ask for it. Jesus Christ made it a principle that when you pray, believe that you have received and you will see the results. Our confidence is in the name of Jesus. The name of Jesus is our power of attorney to do anything and have anything. The Bible says “Therefore God has highly exalted Him and given Him a name that is above every name, that at the mention of the name of Jesus, every knee should bow of things in heaven and things on earth, and of things under the earth”- Philippians 2:9-10.



When you pray in the name of Jesus, you receive your answers at the point of prayer. God doesn’t say to you, “come back later and I’ll let you know if you have received”, it is at the point of your prayer. When you pray for your children, your finances, the church or any other thing, you receive your answers at the point of prayer. Whether you feel like it has been answered or not, you have already received it, wait for the manifestation.


Embrace the privilege and power of praying henceforth with the consciousness that you always receive. Your assurance is the name of Jesus.


Confession

I always receive whatever I ask for when I ask. I decree victory in every area of my life that I am struggling with. I receive faith in my heart to not doubt what God says concerning my life. Amen.*
